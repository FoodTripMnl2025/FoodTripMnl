Welcome to FoodTrip MNL

Food Trip MNL is a Filipino food business established on March 6, 2024, by its owner, John Gutierrez. It is located at 2770 Benita Street, Tondo, Manila, and operates from Monday to Friday, 4:00 PM to 2:00 AM. The business specializes in Filipino food, offering best-selling dishes such as Bulalo, Sizzling meals, Pares, and a variety of Silog meals.

Food Trip MNL aims to serve affordable meals, catering to students, workers, and local residents, especially during nighttime hours. With its budget-friendly pricing and large servings, the business continues to attract customers looking for quality Filipino dishes at reasonable prices.

Aside from its delicious meals, Food Trip MNL also prides itself on providing a cozy and welcoming atmosphere where customers can unwind after a long day. The business plans to expand its menu and improve its services to better serve its growing customer base. With continuous support from the community, Food Trip MNL envisions becoming one of the go-to food spots in Tondo, Manila for satisfying Filipino comfort food.
